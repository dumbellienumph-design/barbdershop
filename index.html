Our Story

My New Barber Shop

My name is Muhammad Aslam, and I've been cutting hair in Upington for years. I started this shop with one simple promise — everyone who sits in my chair walks out looking their best, no exceptions.

Located at Shop no.4, 29 Schroder Street, we specialise in sharp fades, precision tapers, beard grooming, hot towel shaves, facials, and nose & ear waxing. From a 10-year-old's first proper cut to a gentleman's luxury shave — we handle every age, every style, every time.

Walk in, settle into our leather lounge, and leave your look to us. We don't rush. We don't cut corners. We just cut hair — and we do it well.

Why Choose Us

What We Stand For

Precision Every Time

From skin fades to taper blends, we take our time and get it right. No rushing, no corners cut — just clean, consistent work every single visit.

Friendly Service

We keep the atmosphere easy and welcoming. Whether you're a regular or walking in for the first time, you'll always feel at home in our chair.

Honest Prices

Quality cuts shouldn't cost a fortune. Our prices are fair, upfront, and reflect the work that goes into every service. No surprises at the till.

Great with Kids

We're patient, calm and experienced with young ones. Kids under 12 get their own special pricing and the same focused attention as every adult client.

Full Grooming Menu

From buzz cuts to hot towel straight razor shaves, our full menu covers every grooming need. One shop, everything you need to look your best.

Rooted in Upington

We're a local business serving our community. Every cut supports a Upington-based team that genuinely cares about the people who walk through our door.
